Movie Summary

Interstellar is a science fiction movie directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet to save humanity from extinction. The movie beautifully combines science, emotions, and space exploration.

Actors

The movie stars Matthew McConaughey as Cooper, Anne Hathaway as Amelia Brand, Jessica Chastain as Murph, and Michael Caine as Professor Brand.
